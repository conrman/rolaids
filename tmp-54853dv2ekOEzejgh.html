What is Acid Indigestion?

Like heartburn, acid indigestion can cause burning and pain in the upper abdomen. And like heartburn, when it occurs on a regular basis, it can interfere with your enjoyment of life. What are the symptoms of Acid Indigestion

Indigestion is not the same as heartburn, even though excess stomach acid causes both. Indigestion is the discomfort in your stomach, while heartburn is a bit higher in your esophagus.

Indigestion after a meal is a painful situation, and can include bloating, nausea, queasiness, and a feeling of fullness. What do you do? What else? Kick acid, with Rolaids®!
